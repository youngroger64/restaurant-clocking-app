How to demo the app

  1. Upload or confirm the weekly roster.
  2. Click Create Demo Week.
  3. Open Payroll Issues and fix the few exceptions.
  4. Open Weekly Payroll and check that the week is ready.
  5. Export the Sage CSV.

The point of the demo is simple: the manager fixes exceptions, not timesheets.
